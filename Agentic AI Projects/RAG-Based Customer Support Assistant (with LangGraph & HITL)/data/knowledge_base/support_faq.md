# Support FAQ

Q: Where can I find my order history?
A: Open the account dashboard and go to My Orders.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard prepaid delivery is usually 3 to 5 business days.

Q: What should I do if I did not get my reset email?
A: Check spam, confirm your registered email address, and request a new reset link.

Q: When does a risky billing case get escalated?
A: Fraud, legal, and chargeback cases should be handed to a human agent.
